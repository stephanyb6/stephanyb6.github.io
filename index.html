Hello my name is Sephany Balderas and I am a 3rd year Biology major. I am interested in biology because it helps us understand the life processes. Biology allows individuals to unravel the mysteries of life by studying the processes that occur within living organisms. This includes processes such as metabolism, reproduction, growth, and adaptation. Another thing that draws me to biology is that it has a Medical Application. Biology plays a crucial role in the field of medicine. Understanding biological processes helps in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. Researchers in biology contribute significantly to advancements in medical science, leading to improved healthcare practices. Due to me wanting to become a doctor that specializes in pediatrics, it is safe to sy that biology is my calling.

My experience in the medical field has been very interesting. Being involed in the medical field gives me the opportunity to interact with patients on a daily basis. This includes providing care, communicating diagnoses, and offering support to individuals and their families during challenging times. Developing strong interpersonal skills is crucial in the medical field. One of my favorite things about this field is Medical knowledge is constantly evolving, and individuals in the medical field need to engage in lifelong learning to stay abreast of advancements. This involves attending conferences, participating in workshops, and staying updated on the latest research and medical practices.
